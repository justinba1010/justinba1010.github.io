Good Will Hunting

Summary of Good Will Hunting

Good Will Hunting is a great movie about Matt Damon. He starts off as a janitor at MIT. He anonymously solves all the very difficultproblems put on the board. A professor decides to help him take his life in the right direction and along the way he meets a girl.

End of Summary

My Thoughts

This movie is a classic for me. I watched it for the first time in my psychology class 3 years ago. Since then I've probably watched it 10 times and it never gets old. I consider this movie one of my all time favorite movies.
